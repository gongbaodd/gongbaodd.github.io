# 错题整理

$$
y=f(x)=\ln(x+\sqrt{x^2+1})
$$

求$f^{-1}(x)$

答

$$
-y  = -\ln(x+\sqrt{x^2+1}) \\
    = \ln{\frac{1}{x+\sqrt{x^2+1}}} \\
    = \ln{\frac{x-\sqrt{x^2+1}}{(x+\sqrt{x^2+1})(x-\sqrt{x^2+1})}} \\
    = \ln{(\sqrt{x^2+1}-x)} \\
e^y =     \sqrt{x^2+1} + x \\
e^{-y} =  \sqrt{x^2+1} - x \\
e^y-e^{-y} = 2x \\
y = f^{-1}(x) = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2}
$$
